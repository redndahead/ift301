What We Do

Greene Scholars receive year-round hands-on math, science and technology enrichment; compete in annual science fairs and engineering competitions; participate in entrepreneurial/leadership programs; and receive college application support.

Why It Works

Greene Scholars provides an environment for students of African descent to build a supportive community who will leave high school college ready, STEM-competent and STEM-confident.

Get Involved

Discover how you can get involved in Greene Scholars. Through donations, volunteers, and institutional partnerships Greene Scholars is able to provide amazing opportunities for the scholars.

About Us

Our leadership team is made up of dedicated individuals that are tirelessly dedicated to supporting the mission of the Greene Scholars program.

Our interest in science and math is consistently nurtured. We are encouraged to explore our creativity and innovativeness. Our intellectual curiosity is valued. We ALL graduate from high school and college. We are successful scientists, engineers or professionals with STEM backgrounds poised to lead our country. There are no achievement or opportunity gaps. ALL of our students graduate from high school. 100% of those who successfully complete the program continue their education primarily through 4-year universities, competent and confident in the knowledge of the role STEM plays in their lives.
