Volunteer

Sharing your expertise and experience with Greene Scholars changes how they engage their minds as global innovators. A few hours of your time helps the Greene Scholars Program advance STEM enrichment, leadership training, and program administration.

Join us as we level the playing field to diversify STEM skills and perspectives in a competitive and increasingly shrinking world.
